About Us

Surplus Furniture quickly became a top furniture and mattress retailer with the buying power to provide incredible value at a warehouse price! Our concept is simple; use a low overhead warehouse and offer quality brand name furniture and mattresses, purchased direct from the factory at deep discounts and pass the savings on to the customers. This strategy has provided customers with sofas, loveseats, sectionals, motion furniture, recliners, coffee tables, lamps, TV stands, area rugs, bedrooms, mattresses, dinettes, bunk beds, accent furniture and much more at unbeatable low prices.

With this strong focus, along with great customer service and Same Day Delivery, the dedicated employees of Surplus Furniture and Mattress Warehouse set their sights on growth. Surplus Furniture began to expand to other provinces with locations in New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, PEI and Saskatchewan while continuing to add locations in Alberta, Manitoba, Ontario and Newfoundland.
