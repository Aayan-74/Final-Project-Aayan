FAQs

How can I find a store near me?

Use our store locator to find a nearby location by entering your zip code.

What are your store hours?

Store hours vary by location. Please check your local store listing.

How does your layaway program work?

You can reserve items with a small deposit and pay over time.

How do I add items to my cart?

Click the "Add to Cart" button on any product page.

Curbside pick up?

Select "Curbside Pickup" at checkout and follow the instructions in your confirmation email.

How do I check out?

Click your cart and proceed to checkout. Enter shipping and payment info to complete.

Can I place an order using my phone?

Yes! Our website is mobile-friendly and supports full shopping on phones.

How can I find out if a product is sold in-store or online?

Each product page indicates if it is available online, in-store, or both.

Do you offer rainchecks on products that are out of stock?

Rainchecks are not available at this time for online items.

How do I create an online account?

Click "Sign Up" on the homepage and fill out the registration form.

How do I access my online account?

Click "Login" and enter your email and password to access your account.

I forgot my password, how do I request a new one?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the reset instructions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, and store gift cards.

How do I order online?

Browse products, add to cart, and proceed through checkout to complete your order.
